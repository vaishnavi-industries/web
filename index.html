About

We deal in Automatic Powder Coating. Our firm specializes in surface coatings and well helps you to provide with a state of the art Coating Plant, well worthy of your investment.

We are leading manufacturer of powder coating plant. To set up a Powder Coating Plant various parameters must be kept in mind such as the sizes and shapes of the components, coating quality and thickness of paint required, the production speed, space available and many more. Our firm specializes in surface coatings and well help you to provide with a state of the art Coating Plant, well worthy of your investment.

Our range of Batch Plant facility includes basic batch Plant to conveyorised Plant with auto reciprocators and auto guns. The powder recovery is offered in all denominations from basic mono cyclone to multi cyclones and finally cartridge recovery system. The booths are offered in SS, CRCA-Powder Coated, and CRCA-Liquid Painted as per agreement. Their basic purpose is to collect the over sprayed powder throws off the powder coating gun. The recovery booths are of various types depending upon their increasing efficiency; they are single cyclone, multi cyclone and cartridge type.
